History

Craigslist (stylized as craigslist) is an American classified advertisements website with sections devoted to jobs, housing, for sale, etc. It was founded by Craig Newmark in 1995. It started off as a email distribution list to friends featuring local events in the San Francisco Bay Area. In 1996 it became a web-based service and explanded forming other categories. It began expanding to Canada and other U.S. cities in 2000, and today covers about 70 countries.
